Kayou

Branding work (logo design, logo animation, packaging, and communication) for a subversive brand. ‘Kayou’ aims to sell you a product whose packaging likely costs more than the product itself. The ‘Kayou(x)’ were collected on the beach in Douarnenez and traveled by TGV and TER through Nantes, Quimper, Rennes, and Paris before reaching Amiens.
